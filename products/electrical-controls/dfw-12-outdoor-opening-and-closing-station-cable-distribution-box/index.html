      DFW12 outdoor opening and closing station cable distribution box

      DFW12 outdoor opening and closing station cable distribution box

      Product Overview

      DFW series European-style high-voltage cable branch box is widely used in the node connection of 10KV cable system, and is the main electrical equipment for collecting and branching cables after they come out of the switch cabinet. DFW branch box is designed for outdoor maintenance-free operation, safe and reliable, its protection level is IP33~IP66, and the underground tap can withstand flooding. This product is widely used in urban industrial districts, residential districts, commercial centers, mining areas, large enterprises such as steel, automobile, petroleum, chemical industry, cement and other occasions in the distribution network, especially suitable for urban power grid reconstruction projects, which can greatly save electrical equipment and Cable investment to improve power supply reliability.

      The cable branch box is divided into two categories according to its electrical composition: one type does not contain any switchgear, and there are only accessories for processing and connecting the cable ends in the box. The structure is relatively simple, the volume is small, and the function is relatively single. It can be called an ordinary branch box; the other type is that there are not only the accessories of the ordinary branch box in the box, but also one or more switchgears. High, can be called advanced distribution box.

      Performance characteristics：

      1. Fully insulated, fully sealed, fully protected, and fully working.

      2. The incoming and outgoing lines are flexible, and there are at most 9 branch incoming and outgoing lines in practical applications.

      3. Anti-flood, anti-pollution, anti-condensation, anti-frost, corrosion resistance.

      4. Small size, compact structure, simple installation and easy operation.

      5. A live indicator can be installed to remind the operator that the line is live.

      6. A cable-type fault indicator can be installed, which is convenient for quickly finding cable faults.

      The main technical parameters:

      1. Rated voltage: 12kV

      2. Rated frequency: 50/60Hz

      3. Rated current: 630A

      4. Rated short-time withstand current: 25kV/4s

      5. Rated peak withstand current: 50kA

      6. Rated breaking capacitance current: 45A

      7. Rated breaking capacitor current: 16A

      8. Rated short-circuit switching current: 50kA

      9. Power frequency withstand voltage (phase-to-ground): 42kV/min

      10. Lightning impulse voltage: 75kV
